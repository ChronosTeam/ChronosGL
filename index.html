ChronosGL

A minimal WebGL2 3D Engine written in Dart

About

ChronosGL is:
  • an MIT licensed 3D Engine written in Dart
  • based on WebGL2 and GLSL 3.00es
  • structured into layers. With the core layers kept minimal to make it easy to understand and modify them
  • trying to avoid mechanisms that would reduce the design space of projetcs building on top of it. For example there is no implicit animation loop
  • committed to idiomatic Dart, clean compiles and testing
  • built on top of John McCutchan's excellent vector_math library
    • but has no other heavy dependencies
