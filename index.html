ChronosGL

A minimal WebGL 3D Engine written in Dart

About

ChronosGL is an MIT licensed 3D Engine written in Dart.
It is built on top of John McCutchan's excellent vector_math library.
Its design is minimal to make it easy to understand and modify.
It tries to avoid any mechanisms that would reduce the design space of projetcs building on top of it. For example there is no implicit animation loop.
It aims to use idiomatic Dart and have as much test coverage as possible.
